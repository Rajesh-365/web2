> *"Were not our hearts burning within us?"* — Luke 24:32

A premier theological institution dedicated to forming men and women in the faith through rigorous academic study, spiritual formation, and missional engagement.

---

## 🏛️ About

Emmaus Institute of Theology is committed to equipping students with a deep understanding of Christian theology, biblical studies, and ministry practice. Rooted in the transformative encounter on the road to Emmaus, our institution seeks to kindle hearts and minds for God's kingdom.

---

## 📚 Programs Offered

- **Bachelor of Theology (B.Th.)**
- **Master of Divinity (M.Div.)**
- **Master of Arts in Biblical Studies (M.A.B.S.)**
- **Diploma in Christian Ministry**
- **Certificate in Biblical Languages**
